## 10. Glossary

- **PCE (Personal Consumption Expenditures)**: The Fed's preferred inflation gauge. "Core PCE" excludes food and energy prices. 4.1% means prices rose 4.1% over the past year — above the Fed's 2% target.
- **VIX**: The "fear index." Below 20 = calm market. Above 30 = high fear/volatility. Today's ~19 means investors are slightly more nervous than yesterday but not panicking.
- **RESTRICT posture**: Means no new positions are opened. Existing positions are held with their stops. Not panic-selling — just pausing new buys until the market gives clearer signals.
- **Uptrend ratio (28.7%)**: Of all stocks tracked, 28.7% are in an uptrend. A healthy bull market typically shows 40-60%+. Below 30% is cautious territory.
- **Sector rotation**: Sectors take turns leading the market. Right now Healthcare and Industrials lead; broad Technology lags. For SEC0, what matters most is whether semiconductors specifically are leading within tech — and this week they are.
- **SOXX**: The US iShares PHLX Semiconductor ETF — a proxy for the US semiconductor sector. Similar to SEC0 but US-listed and USD-denominated. Used here as a benchmark for SEC0's likely direction.
- **ISM Manufacturing PMI**: A monthly survey of US manufacturers. Above 50 = expansion (more orders coming in). Below 50 = contraction. High importance for industrial and technology stocks.
- **NFP (Non-Farm Payrolls)**: The monthly US jobs report. The single most market-moving economic release. Strong jobs = Fed more likely to keep rates high = pressure on growth and tech stocks.
- **Two-consecutive-close rule**: Alessandro's personal exit rule for SEC0 — the ETF must close below €18.50 for TWO consecutive days before exiting. Single-day dips below the stop don't trigger a sale.
- **CB2 (Circuit Breaker 2)**: An automatic confidence cap applied when more than 3 data sources fail. Today's analysis is missing breadth CSV, macro regime data, and XETRA quotes. Confidence is capped at 45% as a result.
- **HBM4 (High Bandwidth Memory 4)**: The newest generation of AI memory chips, used in NVIDIA's AI GPU clusters. Micron reported HBM4 is sold out through 2026 — direct confirmation that AI chip demand is structural, not speculative.
- **Qualcomm Investor Day**: An annual event where Qualcomm presents its long-term revenue targets to investors. The Jun 24 event was highly bullish for the semiconductor sector — raising targets significantly above prior guidance.
