## 11. Glossary

| Term | Plain-English Meaning |
|------|-----------------------|
| **VIX** | The "fear gauge" — measures how worried the market is about the next 30 days. Below 20 = calm, above 30 = fear. Today: 16.23 = calm. |
| **VIX9D** | Same as VIX but covering only the next 9 days. At 22.14 vs VIX30D at 20.25 — traders are buying FOMC-specific protection. |
| **FOMC** | Federal Open Market Committee — the group inside the Federal Reserve that decides US interest rates. Meets 8 times/year. TODAY is the meeting. |
| **Dot Plot** | A chart the Fed publishes 4 times/year where each member marks where they think rates will be. Today's dot plot may show no more 2026 cuts, which is hawkish (bad for growth stocks). |
| **52w High / 52w Low** | The highest and lowest price a stock reached in the past 52 weeks (one year). AMD's 52w high of $558.37 was set ~June 15, 2026. |
| **Breadth** | How many stocks in the market are going up vs down. Today's uptrend ratio is 28.6% — only ~800 of 2,800 tracked stocks are in uptrends. Low but trending up. |
| **Sector Rotation** | When investors move money from one industry (like tech) to another (like financials). Currently happening against your PIE AI portfolio. |
| **Stop Loss** | A pre-set price where you sell to prevent bigger losses. Alessandro's is 7-8% per position. AMD hit exactly 7.3% down in one day — it's AT the stop zone. |
| **Bullish / Bearish** | Bullish = expecting prices to rise. Bearish = expecting prices to fall. |
| **Yield** | The interest rate return on a bond. Higher yields make bonds more attractive vs stocks. Bad for growth stocks (NVDA, AMD, ASML). |
| **Bear Steepening** | When long-term interest rates rise faster than short-term ones. Bad for high-P/E tech stocks as future earnings get discounted more heavily. |
| **P/E Ratio** | Price divided by Earnings. AMD at P/E >100x means you pay $100 for $1 of annual profit — only justified if growth is very high and predictable. |
| **Insider Selling** | When company executives sell their own stock. CEO Lisa Su selling $57M near the 52w high is a caution signal. Insiders rarely sell at lows. |
| **ARK / Cathie Wood** | ARK Investment Management — a large, high-profile fund in tech/innovation. When ARK sells, it often moves stocks. Sold 141K AMD shares on June 15. |
| **XLK** | SPDR Technology Select Sector ETF — tracks US tech stocks broadly. Dropped -2.79% on June 16 vs Dow at record. Direct proxy for your PIE AI sector. |
| **Broadening Regime** | A macro phase where gains spread from mega-caps to small caps and cyclicals. Healthy long-term, but tech may underperform temporarily. |
| **Uptrend Ratio** | Percentage of tracked stocks currently in uptrend. Today: 28.6% of 2,800 stocks — low but the trend direction is improving (up). |
| **CME FedWatch** | Chicago Mercantile Exchange tool showing probability of rate changes at each Fed meeting, based on futures. Currently: 70% prob of hike by year-end 2026. |
| **Composite Confidence** | Alessandro's own scoring of how reliable today's briefing is, capped at 92%. Today: 60% — capped due to FOMC binary risk. |
